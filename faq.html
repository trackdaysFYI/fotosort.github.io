Frequently Asked Questions

What is FotoSort?

FotoSort is a tool that sorts a collection of motorcycle trackday photos by rider.

How does FotoSort work?

After uploading a folder of photos, our proprietary algorithms analyze and group all motorcycles/riders that are found, regardless of angle or viewing perspective (e.g. front, back, side). After processing, the automatic groupings can be refined with our interactive user interface.

A Windows or macOS script is then generated to organize the original photos on your device based on the final groupings, which creates a unique folder for each rider and copies each photo to its appropriate folder.

Why use FotoSort?

Simplify photo browsing: Allow customers to easily find their photos without the hassle of sifting through thousands of images.

Increase sales potential: Improve visibility for photos that customers are interested in purchasing.

Save time and reduce turnaround: Eliminate hours of manual photo sorting, reducing the time until customers can view their photos.

Are photos uploaded to FotoSort secure?

Yes. All uploaded photos are encrypted and authentication is required to access them.

We will never share or distribute your photos.

Can FotoSort handle large volumes of photos?

FotoSort is designed to process up to 5,000 photos per session.

However, if possible, we recommend breaking up sessions in manageable chunks of 50-80 unique riders for the best experience.

This can be done by splitting photo sets by rider session (e.g. Advanced/Intermediate/Novice) before upload, if applicable.

How long does processing take?

Sessions containing up to 1,000 photos are usually processed within 20 minutes after upload.

Excess photos are processed at an approximate rate of 15 minutes per 1,000 photos.

Do I need to keep the FotoSort application open while sessions are processing?

No. After a session is uploaded, processing is done on our secure servers. You may exit the application and return at a later time.

Can I upload new sessions while previous sessions are still processing?

Yes, new sessions can be created at any time, even when older sessions are still processing.

Multiple sessions can also be uploaded at the same time (in parallel) using multiple browser tabs or windows.

Should I resize my photos before uploading them?

No, the FotoSort platform automatically resizes your photos to a size appropriate for uploading and processing.

How does FotoSort handle the same person riding multiple motorcycles, or multiple people riding one motorcycle?

Our advanced algorithms consider the visual similarity of both motorcycles and their riders at the pixel level.

Automatic groupings can be easily refined on our interactive user interface to meet your grouping preferences.

System Requirements

FotoSort is compatible with modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.

Photos must be uploaded from a Windows, macOS, or Linux device with permissions to run Command Prompt or Shell scripts.

An internet connection with at least 2 Mbps upload speed per 1,000 photos is recommended.
