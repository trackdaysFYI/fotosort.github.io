FotoSort

What is FotoSort?

FotoSort is an AI tool to group motorcycle photos by rider, designed for trackday and racing photographers.

How does FotoSort work?

1. Select

Select up to 5,000 unsorted photos for grouping.

FotoSort works with all motorcycle types, including sport bikes, naked bikes, supermotos, and dirt bikes.

2. Process

Our proprietary, custom-built algorithms detect each of the relevant, in-focus riders within each photo, and automatically group them based on visual characteristics such as motorcycle type, color, and rider apparel, regardless of viewing perspective (eg: front/back/side).

3. Review

Use our advanced UI to quickly refine automatic groupings with drag-and-drop functionality, and easily assign a name to each group.

4. Apply

Generate a script that copies your original photos to their designated folders with the click of a button.

FotoSort works on Windows, MacOS, and Linux.

What are the system requirements?

FotoSort works on nearly every Windows, macOS, and Linux device, and does not require the installation of any software.

We recommend using a modern browser (ex: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, etc) and an internet connection with 2+ Mb/s upload for every 1,000 photos uploaded at a time.

Are photos uploaded to FotoSort secure?

Yes. All uploaded photos are encrypted and authentication is required to access them.

We will never share or distribute your photos.

Should I resize my photos before uploading them?

No. FotoSort automatically resizes a copy of your photos prior to upload to reduce upload time.

How many photos should I sort at a time?

FotoSort has been tested to sort up to 5,000 photos at a time.

For the best experience, we recommend splitting uploads in manageable chunks of 30 to 60 unique riders. This can be done by breaking up photos by rider session (Advanced/Intermediate/Novice) or race class (EX, AM, middleweight, etc) prior to upload.

FotoSort performs best when there are photos of each rider across a full range of perspectives.

How long does processing take?

Processing typically takes around 15 minutes for every 1,000 photos in a session.

Do I need to keep FotoSort open during processing?

No. After creating a session, processing is done on our secure servers. You may close FotoSort and return at a later time.

Can I create new sessions while older sessions are still processing?

Yes. New sessions can be created at any time, even when older sessions are still processing.

Multiple sessions can also be uploaded at the same time (in parallel) using multiple browser tabs or windows.
